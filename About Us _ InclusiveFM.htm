About Us

 

Inclusive FM has been setup as a Community Internet visual station and its aim is to give a voice to the socially excluded groups, access skills training and other services, and provide a valuable alternate platform for community and public sector organisations to broadcast their information.

What is Inclusive FM?

As a community visual station, we aim to provide the community with a voice and a platform to highlight among other things, various issues, cultural norms, and the growth of the borough, along with access to media resources, training and employment opportunities.

Our offers from year to year will include:

1) Broadcasting to the communities in North London 2) Internet radio and TV that can be access via broadband anywhere around the world 3) Live uploads of community events that link refugees and other communities with their families abroad. 4) Provide commercial media services to business in North London to promote to their services and increase their viability and sustainability and more…
This website was developed and designed by foydigital with the aim to plan, build and showcase a website with means inclusive to all. Inclusive for all the members of the community in North London who wish to broadcast to the world.
